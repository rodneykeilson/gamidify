F.A.Q

Here's some frequently asked questions.

Question: What is Kingdom Archives?

◄

Answer: Kingdom Archives is a community project. It serves as a resource for people to learn about VALORANT's lore. It also allows artists to get references from the various agents, abilities or weapons found in VALORANT. We are not endorsed by Riot Games.

Question: I found a mistake/I have suggestions about the website.

◄

Answer: If you find mistakes or you have suggestions about the website, you can reach us through the feedback form or through our Discord server.

Question: Why is %language% not available on the website?

◄

Answer: We are planning on supporting every language that VALORANT has a voice over for. If it is not available right now, it means we are probably still working on it. We might need your help so refer to the next question if you wish to help us with it.

Question: I want to contribute to Kingdom Archives.

◄

Answer: First of all, thanks for thinking of helping us! We appreciate it. Now if you wish to help us work on Kingdom Archives, please reach out on our Discord server. We will get in touch and see how best you can contribute to Kingdom Archives.

Question: Can I access removed content from VALORANT here?

◄

Answer: We aren't supporting the removed content as we believe it was removed for a reason. That being said we are thinking about how we could handle bringing back some of that removed content to the website for preservation.
